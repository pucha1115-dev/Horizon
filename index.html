About

Our Mission, History and Our Team

After 25 years of experience in aviation, and having worked with over 45 different nationalities as airline captain, instructor pilot, and fleet quality control manager, Roel created HORIZON Languages with the aim of combining highly effective aviation teaching methods with the CEFR language framework.

Roel is supported by Steffen, who studied different branches of economics, yet found his true passion in teaching, and changed his university studies accordingly. Steffen has extensive, multi-cultural experience in in-person and online teaching, and can truly be regarded a master in the German language.

Our mission at HORIZON Languages is to deliver an unparalleled quality in language training. We integrate student performance tracking in our courses, and deliver a continuous predictive analysis of outcome, in order to maximise your Return on Investment.

Synergy based values, integrity, and transparency are core values within our organisation.

Our Offer

At HORIZON Langauges, we care about your Return On Investment.

We have created a virtual, AI-enhanced, eLearning whiteboard, aimed at making language learning easier, and faster than traditional methods, without compromising on teacher-student interaction.

Having restructured the traditional module hours allocation, the learning experience is optimised, a stronger foundation is secured, and exposure to the language is increased. Student effort and performance are continuosly monitored, in order to predict exam outcome.

Should you or your students not make the progress that we promise, we offer a partial credit back in order to protect your Return On Investment.

Our current product range is German CEFR levels A1 to C2, as well as conversational training for professionals and beginners alike.

Our Pricing

Long-term success is primarily dependent on exposure to the language, and therefore optimal hour allocation per course module.

Our prices are based on the most common goal of attaining B2 proficiency, with a total of 550 hours or 40 weeks (allowing for public holidays) of learning, at 3 hours per day.

Intensive Course – 3 hours per day

PHP 15,500 per learner per month with a minimum course participation of seven learners.

Super-Intensive Course – 6 hours per day

PHP 30,000 per learner per month with a minimum course participation of seven learners.

Should you require a different proficiency level, and therefore a different course structure, we will be glad to offer you a detailed price based on your needs.
